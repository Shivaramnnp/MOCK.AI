Sample LaTeX Questions for Testing:

1. Math Equation:
   Question: What is the solution to the quadratic equation $x^2 + 5x + 6 = 0$?
   Options:
   A. $x = -2$ and $x = -3$
   B. $x = 2$ and $x = 3$
   C. $x = -1$ and $x = -6$
   D. $x = 1$ and $x = 6$

2. Physics Formula:
   Question: According to Einstein's mass-energy equivalence, what does $E = mc^2$ represent?
   Options:
   A. Energy equals mass times the speed of light squared
   B. Energy equals mass divided by the speed of light squared
   C. Energy equals mass plus the speed of light squared
   D. Energy equals mass minus the speed of light squared

3. Chemistry Equation:
   Question: What is the balanced chemical equation for photosynthesis?
   Options:
   A. $6CO_2 + 6H_2O \rightarrow C_6H_{12}O_6 + 6O_2$
   B. $CO_2 + H_2O \rightarrow C_6H_{12}O_6 + O_2$
   C. $6CO_2 + 6H_2O \rightarrow C_6H_{12}O_6 + O_2$
   D. $CO_2 + H_2O \rightarrow C_6H_{12}O_6 + 6O_2$

4. Calculus:
   Question: What is the derivative of $f(x) = x^3 + 2x^2 - 5x + 1$?
   Options:
   A. $f'(x) = 3x^2 + 4x - 5$
   B. $f'(x) = 3x^2 + 4x + 5$
   C. $f'(x) = 3x^2 - 4x - 5$
   D. $f'(x) = 3x^2 - 4x + 5$

5. Display Mode Equation:
   Question: What is the Pythagorean theorem?
   Options:
   A. $$a^2 + b^2 = c^2$$
   B. $$a + b = c$$
   C. $$a^2 - b^2 = c^2$$
   D. $$ab = c^2$$

6. Greek Letters:
   Question: In trigonometry, what does $\sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1$ represent?
   Options:
   A. The fundamental trigonometric identity
   B. The Pythagorean theorem in trigonometric form
   C. The sum of angles in a triangle
   D. The definition of tangent

7. Fractions:
   Question: What is the value of the integral $\int_0^1 x^2 dx$?
   Options:
   A. $\frac{1}{3}$
   B. $\frac{1}{2}$
   C. $\frac{2}{3}$
   D. $1$

8. Complex Equation:
   Question: Which of the following represents the quadratic formula?
   Options:
   A. $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$
   B. $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 + 4ac}}{2a}$
   C. $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{4ac - b^2}}{2a}$
   D. $x = \frac{b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$

Usage:
1. Copy any question and its options
2. Use the "Prompt" input source in the app
3. Paste the LaTeX content
4. The app will render the LaTeX equations properly!
